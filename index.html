Features You'll Love.

Even with interconnectivity throughout the tech industry, we still have to go through a lot of unnecessary trouble to share the smallest of texts, images or files. No more!

01.

Cross-platform

With an Android app and a PWA(Web App), you can now use this utility across all your devices. And using the power of Flutter, IOS and desktop builds might just be around the corner too!

02.

Open Source

The code is open sourced, as of now, and that means there is no limit to what this could become! Raise whatever issues you have and help solve some for others by submitting PRs!

03.

Free of cost

The application and the web app, are completely free as of now and contain NO ADS. You can make the complete use of this app without any annoying ads bugging you!
